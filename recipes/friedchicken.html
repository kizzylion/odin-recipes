Steps for Preparing our Fried Chicken

This delicacy needs little to no introduction as most of us are really familiar with it but this recipe will probably be the easiest and the best crispy fried chicken legs recipe ever because it is less intimidating when compared to most fried chicken recipes, it uses few simple ingredients, and the result is an extremely crispy and crunchy skin, with a meat that is moist and tender.

  1. First, make a mixture of milk, eggs, salt, and black pepper. Set aside.

  2. Next, combine the flour with the spices. Mix well.

  3. Place the legs inside the dry ingredient mixture and coat well. Then transfer it to the milk mixture. Coat well.

  4. Finally, transfer the legs back to the bowl of dry ingredients and coat well until the legs become thickly coated. At this point, the chicken is ready to be fried.

  5. you want the pieces extra crispy, redip in the egg-milk mixture and seasoned flour.

  6. Fry these battered chicken legs preheated oil in it. The oil in the pan must reach 350 degrees for optimal crisping of the fried chicken batter. You’ll turn the chicken over partway through the frying process until the entire drumstick is crisp and browned—about 12 to 15 minutes.

  7. Be sure to place your fried chicken on a plate lined with paper towels to soak up the extra oil and allow your fried chicken rest for 1-2 minutes before serving.

  8. Serve with a beautifuly and wonderfully made Nigeria Jollof Rice
